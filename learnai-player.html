How AI thinks: tokens & prediction
🎓 Core concept ⏱ 8 min 🔰 Beginner

Discover how large language models actually work under the hood. You'll learn what tokens are, how the model predicts the next word, and why this simple mechanism produces surprisingly intelligent-seeming behaviour.

📌 Key takeaways
Tokenization: AI doesn't read words — it reads fragments called tokens. "ChatGPT" is 3 tokens.
Next-token prediction: Every LLM is fundamentally doing one thing — guessing the most probable next token given all previous tokens.
Temperature: Controls randomness. Low temp = predictable, high temp = creative. This is why the same prompt gives different answers.
Context window: The "memory" of a model. Once exceeded, it forgets earlier parts of the conversation.
